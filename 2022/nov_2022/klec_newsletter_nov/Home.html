LOTTE WORLD BUSAN

When people speak of Lotte World, they often refer to the old one in Seoul. Did you know that Lotte opened a new Lotte World in Busan?

Lotte World Busan was opened just this year on March 31st and I had the good fortune to visit it in August this year.

The Rides

The rides here follow a "Giant" theme. The top 3 rides here are called Giant Splash, Giant Dagger and Giant Swing.

G​iant Dagger

Giant Swing

Giant Splash

Since it is a new theme park, all of the facilities were really new and the queues were really short because not many people know that it has opened. Also, it is located further away from the city centre so that helps too !

The waiting time is only 10 minutes long for the Giant Swing! (130 is the minimum height required)

The Food

There are snack bars all around the park and the food tastes pretty good too!

Uniform Shops

Going to theme parks dressed in school uniforms is a thing in Korea so the uniform shops that have sprung up are really aesthetic!

It only costs KRW20,000 to rent it for a day and it includes transport to and fro Lotte World Busan from the uniform shop.
